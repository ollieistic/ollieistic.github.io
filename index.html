My Background

It all started when I was around 11 years old and discovered how much I enjoyed customizing things — whether it was changing the look of a game, tweaking settings, or making something feel more personal and unique. There was something really satisfying about putting my own spin on things, and it didn’t take long before I realized I had a genuine interest in creating stuff from scratch. Not long after, I started getting curious about game development. I wasn’t exactly sure where to start, but the idea of making my own games or even learning how to code fascinated me. At first, it was just a casual interest — I’d mess around with basic tools and watch tutorials here and there, but I didn’t make a ton of progress right away. It wasn’t until I turned 13 that everything started to click. That’s when I began taking it more seriously and diving deeper into scripting, experimenting with different platforms, and actually building small games and systems on my own. I found myself constantly wanting to improve, to learn more, and to see what I could create next. Since then, what started as a simple curiosity has turned into a full-blown passion. Game development and coding have become something I genuinely love doing — not just as a hobby, but as a way to express creativity, solve problems, and bring ideas to life. And the best part is, I’m still learning and growing every day.
